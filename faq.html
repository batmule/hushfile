Which encryption is used ? Is it safe ?
The file it's metadata are both encrypted with AES-256 in CBC mode with PKCS7 padding. The actual encryption is performed by the CryptoJS 3.1.2 library. From their website:

CryptoJS is a growing collection of standard and secure cryptographic algorithms implemented in JavaScript using best practices and patterns. They are fast, and they have a consistent and simple interface.
